- **üé≠ Cinematic Screenplay Format**: Each crew member presents in full screenplay format with character motivations

- **üéØ Agile Methodology**: Functions like a traditional stand-up but with AI crew members
- **üé™ Immersive Experience**: Full cinematic presentation with scene descriptions and character development
